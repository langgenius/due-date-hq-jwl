# Reply playbook — handling what comes back

Replies land in your inbox (gigi@duedatehq.com → Gmail). Reply from **gigi@duedatehq.com**
so your signature carries. Keep it short and human — same voice as the outreach.
Forward any "no thanks" / bounce to Claude → goes in `outreach-suppress.txt` before the next touch.

---

### 1. Interested / "tell me more" / "how does it work?"
> Glad it landed. Quickest way to see it: open duedatehq.com, add the states you file in,
> and it starts watching them. Happy to hop on 15 min and set it up with you if that's easier —
> what's your week look like?

### 2. "How much is it?" / pricing
> Free while we're in beta — genuinely, no card. We'll price it fairly later, and early
> firms like yours help us figure out what's fair. For now I'd just love your read on whether
> it saves you real time.

### 3. "Not now / it's busy season" / "circle back"
> Totally fair. It's free and sitting there whenever you want a look — no rush. I'll check
> back after the crunch. Good luck with the deadlines.

### 4. "Do you cover [state] / [FEMA] / [my situation]?"
> Yes — IRS, all 50 states + DC, and FEMA disaster postponements. Add your states at
> duedatehq.com and you'll see exactly what's moving for each. If there's a source you rely
> on that we're missing, tell me — we add them.

### 5. "Who are you / is this legit?" / "What's Dify?"
> Fair question. I'm a co-founder — DueDateHQ is a new product from Dify (dify.ai), a company
> that builds AI software. This is a real tool, free in beta; I'm reaching out to a small set
> of firms whose work fits it. Happy to show you live.

### 6. "No thanks" / "unsubscribe" / "remove me"
> No reply needed (a bounce-back reply just annoys). Forward it to Claude → suppressed.
> If you *do* want to reply: "Done — you won't hear from me again. Best of luck this season."

---

**Tone rules:** never oversell, never argue, never chase after a soft no. A CPA who feels
respected on the "no" is a CPA who might come back next season. Answer the actual question,
offer the one next step, stop.
