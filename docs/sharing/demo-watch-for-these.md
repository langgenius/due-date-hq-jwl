# Watch for these — 5 design moments in the demo

_A one-card guide from Yuqi (design). Each of these is deliberate, not accidental._

1. **Gray is the calm color — not green.**
   Everything that's fine recedes into gray, so your eye goes straight to risk.
   Urgency is carried by _size and color_, never bold-on-red. (Green is reserved
   for "filed / done.")

2. **The status pill is not a dropdown.**
   On a deadline, status is _observed_ — it advances from real events. What you
   see instead is the trigger and whatever's blocking it. Nobody hand-picks a
   state.

3. **An alert is a decision, not a notification.**
   The whole alert page funnels to one action: here's the change, here's your
   call, here's who it hits.

4. **Every AI fact shows its source.**
   Look for the little source chips. The rule is _no provenance, no render_ — if
   we can't cite it, we say "not verified yet" instead of guessing.

5. **The same number everywhere.**
   A client's count / date / label reads identically on every surface. That's a
   consistency contract working — not a coincidence.
