Glass-box, by design

Every number on the screen clicks back to its source.

We call it glass-box. The software can summarize, flag, and draft — but it never shows you a date without its source: a link, the exact quote, and a verified date. When the source is missing, DueDateHQ asks you to verify it instead of guessing. You stay the professional in the loop; it just makes the source impossible to lose.

Your clients' data stays in your own isolated practice on Cloudflare — never pooled across firms — and it's email-first, so there's no portal to configure or vault to migrate.

Not tax advice. DueDateHQ supports evidence-backed review. It is not tax advice, not a filing system, and not a replacement for a CPA, EA, attorney, or other qualified professional. It tells you where to look and why; you make the call.
