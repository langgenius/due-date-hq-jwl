Design notes (so a regen stays consistent):
- Faithful to the **live product** — real `StatusRing` geometry, parsed-field grid,
  Change/Source/Activity anchors, tilegram (`US_JURISDICTION_TILES`). Not invented UI.
- Tokens from `docs/Design/color-palette-2026-06-23.md`: accent navy `#22488C`, status
  inks (`#1E7A47` / `#7A5320` / `#B23A28`), **live/new/monitoring = sky `#3AB3E0`** (not green).
- Headlines all **56px** (uniform across every slide). Type-weight restraint: 600 for
  headline + card titles + primary CTA only; 500 key data; 400 everything else.
- Flat, border-framed cards + soft premium elevation (not glossy). Floating overlay
  chips hang off card **edges over padding** (never covering text). Photo avatars for people.
- "View source ↗" uses an external-link glyph; the paper-plane is only for "Email draft".

Reproducing: `generator.cjs` is a **snapshot** — `OUTDIR` and the avatar path
(`/tmp/ph-avatars`) are absolute to the author's machine. To re-run: point `av64()` at
`./avatars`, set `OUTDIR`, then `NODE_PATH=<repo>/node_modules node generator.cjs`.
Avatars in `avatars/` are **placeholder portraits (randomuser.me)** — swap for real
team/licensed headshots before any paid/long-term public use.

## 2. Product Hunt listing copy

**Tagline (≤60 chars):**
> Catch every tax-deadline change — IRS, 50 states & FEMA.

**Description (recommended — leads with the change, then "we catch it", then act):**
> Tax rules and deadlines change constantly — across the IRS, all 50 states, and FEMA.
> DueDateHQ watches all of them, catches every change, and shows you exactly which clients
> are affected — then applies the new date to their deadlines in one click. Every change
> sourced to the official notice; every action logged for audit.
>
> Built for solo and small CPA practices juggling clients across jurisdictions.

> ⚠️ Soften any absolute claims to what's actually live ("monitor daily" vs "the moment",
> avoid "the only one who sees it"). The defensible claim is **acting** on the change, not
> being the only one who sees it — research tools (Checkpoint, Bloomberg Tax) already push
> change alerts.

## 7. Go-to-market (US CPAs — aggressive but realistic)

PH won't bring CPAs. The reachable, high-intent pool = tens of thousands of solo/small firms —
work it directly and convert hard. Ranked by ROI for a 2-person team:

**Tier 1 — founder-led direct (the engine, start now):**
- Cold outreach at volume — list from state CPA society directories, Google Maps, LinkedIn Sales
  Navigator (Accountant/CPA/Partner, 1–20 employees). Personalized email + DM, lead with pain +
  3-months-free. **Quota: 30–50 personalized touches / founder / day.**
- LinkedIn daily (problem-led posts, DMs to firm owners) — the #1 channel for CPAs.
- Tax Twitter/X (#TaxTwitter) — engage, then DM.

**Tier 2 — communities:** r/taxpros (verified-pro, exactly the buyer) + r/accounting; accounting
FB/Slack/Discord groups; state CPA societies + AICPA. Follow self-promo rules; never spam.

**Tier 3 — compounding:** SEO pages from the deadline data ("2026 tax deadlines by state", "IRS
disaster-relief tracker"); G2/Capterra listings + reviews; accounting media/podcasts/newsletters
(Accounting Today, Journal of Accountancy, Cloud Accounting Podcast, Earmark).

**Tier 4 — leverage:** referral program (free months — word of mouth is huge in a tight
profession); partnerships (bookkeepers, CPA coaches, practice-mgmt tools, state societies).

**Paid:** only after organic shows free→activate→pay. LinkedIn (CPA titles) + Google search
("tax deadline tracking software").

**Make it work:** optimize **activation** (firm connects a client → "it caught something for
me") over vanity signups; don't torch reputation with spam (CPAs talk); plan the 3-months-free →
paid conversion, don't just stack free users who churn.

**Launch-week sprint:** now→Mon build the firm list (500+) + warm LinkedIn/r/taxpros + line up
pilots/supporters + wire the offer + schedule PH for Tue → Tue fire all outreach at US-East
morning + reply to everything → Wed–Fri keep the daily quota, follow up clickers, ask every
activated firm for a referral + a review.
